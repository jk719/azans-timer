Privacy Policy

Azan's Timer - ADHD Timer App for Kids

Last Updated: January 2025

Summary: Azan's Timer is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, share, or sell any personal information. All data stays on your device and your private iCloud account.

Information We Collect

Azan's Timer stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Child's first name - Used only to personalize the app experience
  • Timer session history - Activity name, duration, and completion time
  • App settings - Timer presets, sound preferences
  • Parent PIN - Optional 4-digit code for verification (stored locally)
  • Stars and streaks - Reward tracking for motivation

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, the app uses Apple's iCloud Key-Value Storage to sync your settings and session history across your devices. This data is:

  • Encrypted end-to-end by Apple
  • Stored in your private iCloud account
  • Not accessible by us or any third party
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policy

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Photos, contacts, or any other device data

Third-Party Services

Azan's Timer does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app operates entirely offline except for optional iCloud sync.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for use by children under parental supervision. We are committed to protecting children's privacy:

  • No account creation required
  • No data sent to external servers
  • No social features or external communication
  • No advertisements
  • No in-app purchases

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored using Apple's secure frameworks:

  • UserDefaults - For local settings storage
  • iCloud Key-Value Storage - For cross-device sync (optional)

Data remains on your device and in your private iCloud account. We have no access to your data.

Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Deleting the app from your device
  • Removing app data from iCloud in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Manage Storage

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
